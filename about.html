Khaos (Chaos) was the first of the primordial Greek gods to emerge at the dawn of creation. She was followed quickly by Gaia (Gaea, Earth), Tartaros (the Pit Below) and Eros (Procreation). The idea is where everything starts which is then formed into usable products and services for consumers.
